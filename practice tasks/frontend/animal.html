My name is Nimish, and my favorite animal is the capybara. Capybaras are the largest rodents in the world and can weigh over 60 kg. They are extremely friendly and love relaxing in water!

If you want to learn more about the capybara, check out its Wikipedia page .

Capybaras are awesome because…

  1. They are calm and friendly animals
  2. They are great swimmers
  3. Many animals like to sit on them!

If I were a capybara, I would…

  • Relax in warm water all day
  • Make lots of animal friends
  • Eat fresh grass and fruits
